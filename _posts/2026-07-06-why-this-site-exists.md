Every generation gets a technology that redraws the boundary of what people can do. Ours is intelligence itself — the ability to manufacture understanding, and to point it at every problem that has outlasted us.

This site is a working archive for that project. It runs on two tracks. The **technical** track collects methods, architectures, and open problems on the path to superintelligent systems. The **governance** track collects the standards and institutional designs that let a transformative technology move fast and earn the world's confidence — the seatbelts and crash tests of the field, built by people who love cars.

Everything here is catalogued. Essays and papers carry an ID (this one is `ASI-2026-001`), so anything can be cited, referenced, and found again as the library grows.

More soon.
