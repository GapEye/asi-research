  <h1>About</h1>
</header>

<div class="entry-body">

<p><strong>ASI Research</strong> exists to help bring about the most consequential technology humanity will ever build — and to help it arrive well.</p>

<p>Artificial superintelligence is not a distant abstraction. It is the plausible endpoint of trends already visible in machine learning, and it represents an opportunity without precedent: intelligence applied at scale to disease, poverty, energy, materials, and every problem that has resisted us for lack of understanding. Previous general technologies — the printing press, artificial fertilizer, the automobile, the computer — each expanded human possibility enormously. Superintelligence stands to exceed them all combined.</p>

<p>Transformative technologies deserve serious engineering. Cars got seatbelts and crash testing; fertilizers got handling standards; aviation became the safest way to travel precisely because its builders took reliability seriously. The same will be true here: sensible practice and well-designed institutions are how ambitious technology earns adoption and trust. That is why this site runs on two tracks — <span class="track track-technical">technical</span> for the methods that push capability forward, and <span class="track track-governance">governance</span> for the standards and institutions that let progress move fast and stick.</p>

<p>Today this site is a personal research effort: essays, a growing library of papers and methods, and working notes. In time, it is intended to become the foundation of a company.</p>
