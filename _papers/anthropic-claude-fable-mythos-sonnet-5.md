Anthropic's current model table is a useful snapshot of the agentic frontier.
It describes Claude Fable 5 as next-generation intelligence for long-running
agents, Opus 4.8 for complex agentic coding and enterprise work, and Sonnet 5
as the best speed-intelligence combination.

## Deployment signal

The model overview also notes that Claude Mythos 5 and Mythos Preview are
offered separately for defensive cybersecurity workflows through Project
Glasswing, with invitation-only access.

## ASI relevance

The market is segmenting by capability, risk, and workflow. Long-running
agents, coding agents, and restricted cybersecurity models are now distinct
deployment categories rather than abstract benchmark tiers.
