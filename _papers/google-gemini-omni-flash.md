Gemini Omni Flash is not an ASI paper, but it matters to the capability map. It
is a high-speed multimodal generation model that can create short 720p videos
from text, animate still images, and refine outputs conversationally.

## Why it belongs here

ASI systems will not be text-only. Video, audio, simulation, world models, and
interactive editing all contribute to agents that can model environments,
communicate plans, and test ideas visually.

## ASI relevance

The important signal is a shift toward "any input to any output" systems with
iterative editing loops. That is a foundation for simulation-rich scientific,
robotic, and design workflows.
