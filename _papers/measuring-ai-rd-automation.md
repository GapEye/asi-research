This is governance that technical teams should actually want: measure the
acceleration curve. If AI systems begin automating the work of AI researchers,
capability progress, safety progress, compute allocation, and oversight
capacity can diverge quickly.

## Why it belongs here

ASI strategy needs telemetry. The paper proposes tracking dimensions such as AI
R&D spending share, researcher time allocation, and AI subversion incidents.
Those metrics are imperfect, but they make a rapidly changing research process
visible enough to manage.

## Pro-ASI read

The goal is not to slow research by default. The goal is to know when automated
R&D is becoming a major input to frontier progress, so institutions can
increase evals, security, reproducibility, and deployment confidence at the
same tempo.
