GPT-5.6 Sol belongs in the library because the release is explicitly framed
around long-horizon work. OpenAI describes a new `max` reasoning effort for Sol
and an `ultra` mode that uses subagents for complex work.

## Signals to track

- Agentic coding: OpenAI reports a new state of the art on Terminal-Bench 2.1.
- Scientific work: the post reports stronger GeneBench results than GPT-5.5
  while using fewer tokens.
- Productization: the family separates flagship capability, balanced cost, and
  fast inference into Sol, Terra, and Luna.

## ASI relevance

This is a frontier-model drop where the interesting feature is not just answer
quality. It is the shift toward models that budget deeper reasoning, coordinate
subagents, and operate inside tool-heavy workflows. Those are exactly the
ingredients that make AI R&D automation compound.
