Gemini for Science is a product signal: frontier labs are packaging scientific
reasoning as a workflow, not only as a benchmark. Google frames it as tools and
experiments that expand the scale and precision of scientific exploration.

## Why it matters

The practical ASI stack needs more than one great model. It needs daily-use
research surfaces that connect literature review, code, hypothesis generation,
simulation, and artifact production.

## ASI relevance

Science tooling is where capability becomes throughput. The highest-signal
question is whether these tools shorten real iteration cycles for researchers,
not whether they produce impressive one-off demos.
